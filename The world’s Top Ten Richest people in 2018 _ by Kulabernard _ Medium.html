The world’s Top Ten Richest people in 2018

According to the 32nd annual Forbes list of the world’s billionaires, the top 20 richest people on the planet own almost 13% of all billionaires’ fortunes combined. There were a total of 2,208 billionaires in the ranking, with total worth of $9.1 trillion, up 18% from 2017. Due to Amazon’s soaring stock price, which resulted in one person’s largest one-year rise in fortune ($35 billion) since Forbes began tracking in 1987, Jeff Bezos was named the top billionaire for the first time.

#1 Jeff Bezos Jeff Bezos is the world’s richest person by a long shot, with an incredible net worth of $108 billion. In addition to Amazon, Bezos owns the private space exploration company Blue Origin and the Washington Post. In April 2018, he was named the richest man in modern history, and the richest American ever. After taking out Bill Gates as the richest man in modern history, Bezos saw his net worth jump $34 billion, pushing him from number three to number one. #2 Bill Gates After being crowned as the world’s richest person in 1994, Bill Gates is still the world’s richest person, and is number two on the Forbes list with a net worth of $90 billion. He is the founder and CEO of Microsoft, and is currently on a Leave of Absence from his post. He is now only 23.
